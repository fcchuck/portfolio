The first order of business is to stop the spread of the infection. What are the symptoms and what is the cure?

  1. Is the person eating or attempting to eat other humans?

  2. Does this person have bite marks?

  3. Does the person stink (there is a reason zombies are referred to as stenches), have a vacant look and moan a lot?

  4. Is this your pain in the ass neighbor, who is not actually displaying any of the above mentioned symptoms?
